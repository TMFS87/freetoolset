❓ FAQ

What makes a good email subject line?

The best subject lines are concise (30-50 characters), create curiosity or urgency, and clearly communicate value. Our AI generates a mix of styles so you can choose what fits your brand.

How many subject lines do I get?

Each generation produces 10 subject lines in different styles: curiosity-driven, urgency-based, benefit-focused, and personalized. You can generate up to 5 times per day for free.

Should I A/B test subject lines?

Yes! With 10 variations per generation, you can easily A/B test different styles. Start with 2-3 options and measure open rates to find what resonates with your audience.

Can I use these for any email platform?

Absolutely! The generated subject lines work with Mailchimp, SendGrid, ConvertKit, Gmail, Outlook, and any other email platform. Just copy and paste.
