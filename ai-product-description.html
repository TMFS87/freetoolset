❓ FAQ

How many descriptions can I generate for free?

You can generate up to 3 product descriptions per day for free. The counter resets every 24 hours. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited generations.

What makes a good product description?

A great product description highlights benefits (not just features), uses sensory language, addresses the target audience directly, and ends with a clear call to action. Our AI writer follows these best practices automatically.

Can I use the generated descriptions commercially?

Yes! The content generated by this tool is yours to use for your e-commerce store, marketing materials, or any commercial purpose.

Which tone should I choose?

Professional works well for B2B and technical products. Casual is great for lifestyle brands. Luxury suits premium products. Playful fits fun, youth-oriented brands.
