❓ FAQ

What languages does the word counter support?

The word counter works with any language. It counts words by splitting on whitespace, which works for most languages including English, Spanish, French, German, and more. For languages without spaces (like Chinese or Japanese), it counts characters instead.

Does it update in real-time?

Yes! All statistics update instantly as you type or paste text. There's no need to click any button — the counts refresh on every keystroke.

Can I export the counting results?

You can copy your text using the "Copy Text" button. The statistics themselves are displayed on-screen and can be screenshotted or noted down. A dedicated export feature is planned for a future update.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute. If your text has fewer than 200 words, it will show "<1" minute.
