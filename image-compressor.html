📋 How to Use

  1. Click the upload area or drag & drop your image file.
  2. Adjust the compression quality slider (10%–100%).
  3. Optionally set a max width to resize the image.
  4. Choose output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP) and click "Compress".
  5. Preview the result and download the compressed image.

❓ FAQ

What image formats are supported?

The compressor supports JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output. You can convert between formats — for example, convert a PNG to a smaller JPEG.

Will compression reduce image quality?

JPEG and WebP compression uses lossy compression, so some quality loss occurs. Lower quality settings produce smaller files but more visible artifacts. PNG output is lossless. A quality setting of 70–85% typically provides a good balance.

Is there a maximum file size limit?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's available memory. Most browsers can handle images up to 20–50 MB without issues.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing is done locally in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.
