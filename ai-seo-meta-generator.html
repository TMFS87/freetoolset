❓ FAQ

What is a meta title?

A meta title (or title tag) is the headline that appears in search engine results and browser tabs. It should be under 60 characters and include your primary keyword for best SEO performance.

What is a meta description?

A meta description is a brief summary (under 160 characters) that appears below the title in search results. It influences click-through rates and should include relevant keywords and a compelling call to action.

Why does content type matter?

Different content types require different meta tag strategies. A blog post might use a question-based title, while a product page should highlight benefits. The AI adjusts its approach based on your selection.
