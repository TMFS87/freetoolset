🎨 Color Picker

Pick a color and get its HEX, RGB, and HSL values. Copy any format with one click. Includes shade variations and quick palette.

🔒 All processing happens in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.

📋 How to Use

  1. Click the color input to open the browser's color picker.
  2. Alternatively, click a color from the Quick Palette section.
  3. View the HEX, RGB, and HSL values in the Color Values section.
  4. Click "Copy" next to any format to copy it to your clipboard.
  5. Explore shade variations by clicking any shade swatch.

❓ FAQ

What color formats are supported?

The tool supports three common formats: HEX (e.g., #6366F1), RGB (e.g., rgb(99, 102, 241)), and HSL (e.g., hsl(239, 84%, 67%)). All three are displayed simultaneously and can be copied individually.

Can I export a color palette?

You can copy individual color values using the Copy buttons. The Quick Palette and Shades sections provide curated color collections you can pick from. A full palette export feature is planned for a future update.

Is there a color history feature?

The Shades section shows variations of your currently selected color, which serves as a dynamic palette. A dedicated color history feature is on our roadmap for future development.

How do I convert between color formats?

Simply pick or enter a color in any format, and the tool automatically converts and displays it in HEX, RGB, and HSL simultaneously. No manual conversion needed.
