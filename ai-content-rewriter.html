❓ FAQ

What does variation level mean?

Light makes minor word substitutions while keeping the structure. Moderate rewrites sentences with balanced changes. Heavy completely restructures the text while preserving the core meaning.

Does the AI preserve the original meaning?

Yes! The AI is instructed to keep the core meaning intact while changing sentence structure, word choice, and flow. However, we recommend reviewing the output to ensure accuracy.

Can I use this to avoid plagiarism?

The tool rewrites content to be original, but we recommend using it ethically. Always cite your sources and verify the rewritten content meets your institution's or platform's plagiarism guidelines.

What tones are available?

Professional for business contexts, Casual for informal communication, Academic for research and essays, and Creative for storytelling and marketing copy.
